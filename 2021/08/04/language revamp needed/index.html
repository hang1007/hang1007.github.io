language revamp[^1] needed

The terminology[^2] used in genetics needs a complete overhaul[^3] to break free from[^4] its problematic[^5] past,says Adam Rutherford

UNSHARED[^6] science is of little value.

The whole scientific endeavour[^7] relies on ideas ,methods and data being available to all .The words we use
